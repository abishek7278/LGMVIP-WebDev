Field hockey is a team sport of the hockey family.
Each team plays with ten field players and a goalkeeper,
and must carry a round, hard, plastic hockey ball with
a hockey stick to the rival goal.The game is played globally,
particularly in parts of Western Europe, South Asia,
Southern Africa, Australia, New Zealand, Argentina, and parts of
the United States, primarily New England and the Mid-Atlantic states.
